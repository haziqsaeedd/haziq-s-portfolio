Favourite Tourist Spots

Bora Bora

What this small French Polynesian island may lack in size it makes up for in sheer tropical beauty.
Here, you'll find picturesque beaches, lush jungles and luxurious resorts. The island's extinct volcano,
Mount Otemanu, makes for a great photo-op or challenging hike, and the friendly Bora Bora locals can help
you catch a glimpse of the island's best sights during an off-road excursion. To relax, head to Matira Beach
for crystal-clear water and soft white sand.The very definition of a tropical getaway, blissful Bora Bora
abounds with luxurious resorts, sunny skies, warm waters and friendly locals.
